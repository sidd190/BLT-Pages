How elections work

1

Sign in with GitHub

Your GitHub identity verifies your vote and nomination. One account, one vote per role.

2

Nominate during the window

Nominate yourself or another contributor. Nominations are submitted as GitHub Issues for full transparency.

3

Community votes

Once nominations close, voting opens. Sign in and cast your vote directly on this page — one vote per role, per GitHub account.

4

Admin finalizes results

Donnie (super admin) reviews, removes fraudulent votes, and announces the elected Lead and Co-Lead.
